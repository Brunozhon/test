You'll need:

  • A toolbox of SC-750R Snap Circuts pack

  • 4 1.5V batteries

How to install: Install 4 1.5V batteries into 2 battery holders. When installing a battery, be sure that the spring is compressed straight back, and not bent up, down, or to one side. Battery installation should be supervised with an adult.
